  • Basics

  • Can a user forget to clockout when they are done working ?

    When a user forgets to clock out, an email is sent to him 30 minutes after the end of his last working hour.

  • Are the working hours day / night differentiated ?

    Yes, the night hours are different from the day hours. They are therefore well differentiated to keep the difference of advantage.

  • Does overtime count ?

    Yes, overtime is counted and therefore paid more than regular hours.

  • Do we have to clock out to go eat ?

    Yes, you have to clock out to go to lunch and then clock in again when you return to work.

  • Is travel time during work counted ?

    Yes, travel time during work hours is counted as work time.

  • Is there a maximum limit of consecutive hours worked ?

    Yes, you can only work 5 hours at a time. So you have to clock out every 5 hours maximum to take breaks and clock in again.

  • What if I don't have a computer for clock in or clock out ?

    If you don't have a computer at your workplace you can clock in and clock out with the mobile application or with the NFC card.

  • I am visually impaired how do I clock in / clock out ?

    Thanks to the NFC card you can clock in and clock out !

  • I am visually impaired how do I clock in / clock out ?

  • Mobile

  • How do I clockin / clockout with the mobile app ?

    First you need to log in with your user credential. Then when you start your working time, simply press the "Clock in" button in the screen. When you have finish your work, press the "Clock out" button. That's all !

  • Account

  • How do I change my password?

    For changing your password, simply click on the "Forget password ?" button then you will receive an email with instructions.

  • How do I change my account settings?

    For changing your account settings, go to the settings section of the dashboard. Then go to "account settings".

  • I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

    For changing your password, simply click on the "Forget password ?" button then you will receive an email with instructions.

  • Privacy

  • Who have access to my working times datas ?

    Your working time datas are stocked and visible only for your administrators and your manager.
